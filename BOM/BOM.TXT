U8          1          FM24CL64B           SOIC-8          64Kbit I2C F-RAM, parameter persistence across power
                                                            loss - shares I2C1 with the OLED (U7 only has two I2C
                                                            peripherals total, both already committed elsewhere -
                                                            see PINOUT.TXT's I2C1 block for the full reasoning).
                                                            A0/A1/A2 (chip-select address pins) and WP (write-
                                                            protect) all tied directly to GND - address 0x50
                                                            (doesn't collide with the OLED's own fixed 0x3C), write
                                                            operations always enabled. SOIC-8 confirmed against the
                                                            official Infineon/Cypress datasheet (also available in
                                                            TSSOP - SOIC-8 chosen for this board). F-RAM, not
                                                            EEPROM: no write-cycle delay (writes complete at bus
                                                            speed - "NoDelay" per the datasheet), and endurance in
                                                            the trillions of cycles rather than an EEPROM's typical
                                                            ~1 million - see firmware/README.md section 6.
